# Disclaimer

Gene Prioritizer AI is a **research and educational** decision-support tool.

## What this tool is

- A way to explore candidate genes against a phenotype profile, using the
  Human Phenotype Ontology (HPO), HGNC gene validation, and (in later
  phases) PubMed literature evidence.
- A tool to support — not replace — the judgment of qualified genetics and
  medical professionals.

## What this tool is **not**

- **Not medical advice.**
- **Not a diagnostic device.** No output is, or should be interpreted as,
  a clinical diagnosis.
- **Not a substitute for professional clinical genetics evaluation.**
- **Not automatically HIPAA-compliant.** Do not enter identifiable patient
  information unless you have deployed this application in an environment
  that you have independently confirmed meets your applicable compliance
  obligations (e.g. HIPAA, GDPR, or your institution's policies).

## How outputs should be used

Every score, ranking, or evidence summary produced by this tool — in any
phase of the project — must be treated as a **prioritization score for
research and triage purposes only**, never as a probability of disease or
a diagnostic conclusion. All outputs require review and interpretation by
a qualified genetics or medical professional before they inform any
clinical decision.

## Current build status

This document describes the disclaimer policy for the complete, planned
application (Phases 1-12). As of **Phase 1**, the only functionality that
exists is the application shell, navigation, and this documentation — the
phenotype input, HPO matching, gene validation, ranking, and literature
features described in the architecture contract
(`docs/PHASE_0_ARCHITECTURE.md`) are not yet built. The disclaimer banner
shown on every page already reflects the final policy, ahead of the
features it will apply to.
